ABOUT UPPCH

Organizing Since 2025 —
A Stronger Haiti

UPPCH is a civic movement built on the belief that every Haitian — at home and in the diaspora — has a right to participate in shaping their country's future.

We are a social and political organization committed to advocating for the rights, dignity, and progress of the Haitian people. Through community organizing, civic education, and direct action, we work to address the systemic challenges facing Haiti and its communities abroad.

Our work is grounded in solidarity. From political advocacy to grassroots outreach, UPPCH mobilizes people to push for the reforms and resources that communities in Haiti urgently need.

OUR MISSION

Building a Stronger, United Haiti Together

OUR MISSION OUR VISION OUR GOALS

UPPCH is a social and political organization dedicated to uniting the Haitian community around shared values of justice, progress, and self-determination. Through civic engagement, community organizing, and advocacy, we work to create meaningful and lasting change in Haiti and in Haitian communities worldwide.

Statutes & Declaration of Principles

UPPCH is governed by a formal constitution built on democracy, transparency, and social justice. Discover our fundamental values, party structure, and commitment to the Haitian people.

Declaration of Principles

Founded in 2025, UPPCH is built on four pillars: Youth, Diaspora, Haitian Women, and Farmers — united for a more just and prosperous Haiti.

Democratic Structure

The Congress, National Council, and National Directorate — a three-tier system ensuring every voice in the party is represented.

Member Rights & Duties

Every member has the right to vote, run for office, and participate. Every member has the duty to respect the statutes and contribute to the party's growth.
