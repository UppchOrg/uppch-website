ABOUT UPPCH

Every person deserves to live with dignity, hope, and opportunity — UPPCH is here to make that a reality for the people of Haiti.

UPPCH is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping the most vulnerable communities in Haiti. Through education, health services, and food assistance, we aim to uplift lives and bring lasting change.

Our mission is rooted in compassion and action. Whether it’s school supplies, medical aid, or basic resources, UPPCH works to support families and individuals in need, one step at a time.
